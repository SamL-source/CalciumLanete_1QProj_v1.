Projects & Activities

Every May, we have a two-day event to conduct workshops and art making contests about the ocean and marine life.

Ocean Awareness Workshop Objectives:
  1. Discusses and expounds on the chosen marine issue
  2. Showcases several inventions or solutions that can contribute to cleaning our ocean
  3. Includes engagement activities for our future advocates, inventors, and leaders
  4. MOST IMPORTANTLY, it fosters learning and environmental stewardship to our youngsters!


Poster-making Contest

 This contest occurs on-site, with the theme announced during the event.
Awards:
  1. Champion - Php 2,000 and Certificate
  2. First runner up - Php 1,500 and Certificate
  3. Second runner up - Php 1,000 and Certificate
Sponsors: UP Mindanao and Marine Conservation Philippines

When? May 15-16, 2025
Where? Whispering Tides Resort

Ocean Innovation Program

The Ocean Innovation Program showcases each group’s creations as they defend and convince the panelists to compete for the winning spot. It promotes creativity, problem-solving, critical thinking, and teamwork. This is a one-day event that occurs every two years to give enough time for the planning and creation of the invention.

When? March 29, 2026
Where? Whispering Tides Resort

Awards:
  1. Champion - Php 3,000, Certificate, and gold medal
  2. First runner up - Php 2,500 and Certificate
  3. Second runner up - Php 1,500 and Certificate

Sponsors: UP Mindanao and Marine Conservation Philippines

Community Beach Festival

The Community Beach Festival is a two-day event that consists of enjoyable and creative booths for fundraising and at the same time for building FRIENDSHIPS and having FUN. There are games to play, foods to try, and hands-on activities to interact with.

When? June 15, 2025
Where? Whispering Tides Resort
